## Closing Summary

The FUZE tokenomics structure is built around a fixed total supply of 500,000,000 FUZE distributed across ten purpose-specific categories. The largest buckets are Treasury Reserve, Community Presale, and Holder Incentives, while the wider design preserves clear separation among public distribution, migration continuity, team and contributor alignment, stewardship reserves, ecosystem expansion, market operations, and transparency-oriented reserve support. These tables are intended to function as the compact numerical and structural companion to the wider FUZE whitepaper and system design documentation.
