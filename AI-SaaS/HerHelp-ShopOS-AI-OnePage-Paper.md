# HerHelp ShopOS AI  
## QR Shop OS / Queue POS / Trust-Control POS for Thai Small Shops

### Overview

HerHelp ShopOS AI is a complete QR-based shop operating system designed for Thai small shops, food booths, cafés, takeaway stores, pop-up shops, and product sellers. It turns one simple QR code into a digital menu, checkout system, queue system, payment flow, stock checker, customer relationship tool, loyalty program, delivery order page, and owner dashboard.

The product is built for real small-shop behavior in Thailand, where customers are used to scanning QR codes, shop owners may not always be at the booth, employees often operate the shop, and owners need a simple way to check whether sales, stock, payment, and staff actions match each day.

### Core Problem

Many small Thai shops still rely on manual order taking, paper queues, memory-based customer service, and informal stock counting. This creates common problems:

- Customers wait without knowing their queue.
- Staff must shout queue numbers manually.
- Owners cannot easily check if stock and revenue match.
- Employees may make mistakes with payment, stock, orders, or cash.
- Returning customers lose the personal service feeling when the owner is not present.
- Delivery orders, walk-in orders, and pickup orders are managed separately.
- Small shops cannot afford complex POS systems or expensive hardware.

HerHelp ShopOS AI solves this by giving small shops a low-cost, mobile-first system that works with phones, tablets, QR codes, speakers, monitors, printers, and existing shop devices.

### Customer Flow

Customers scan the shop QR code and open the shop menu. They can browse products, see promotions, read product stories, choose signature or highlight products, add items to cart, select pickup, queue, pre-order, or delivery, then checkout and pay.

After payment succeeds, the system confirms the queue and can announce it by voice:

“คุณปูได้คิวที่ 8 โปรดรอสักครู่นะคะ”

The customer can then track the order status from their phone:

Waiting → Preparing → Ready → Completed

### Shop Operation Flow

For staff, the system shows a live order and kitchen task screen. Each order includes queue number, customer nickname, payment status, product list, notes, customer preferences, and action buttons such as Start Preparing, Ready, Completed, Cancel, and No-show.

For the public display, the shop can show queue number and customer nickname on a TV, tablet, monitor, or old phone. The display can also play voice announcements when the queue is confirmed or the order is ready.

### Trust-Control Advantage

The strongest difference from normal POS systems is the TrustCheck layer.

At the end of each day, the system compares:

Opening stock  
+ Added stock  
- Sold quantity  
- Waste / staff meal / free item  
= Expected remaining stock

Then it compares expected stock with actual counted stock and expected revenue with confirmed payment.

This helps the owner answer one important question:

“Does today’s money match today’s stock?”

This is especially useful for shops where employees operate the booth without the owner onsite.

This helps employees serve regular customers with the same warmth as the shop owner.
