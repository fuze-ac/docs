# HerHelp SpeakShop AI  
## AI-Powered Front-Shop Voice Promoter for Small Businesses

### Overview

HerHelp SpeakShop AI is an AI-powered front-shop speaker system that helps small businesses attract customers automatically. The product turns any mobile phone, tablet, or connected speaker into a smart voice promoter that can speak natural sales messages when people walk past a shop.

Instead of using a fixed recorded message, SpeakShop AI connects shop information, daily promotions, AI-generated sales scripts, and AI voice into one simple system. A shop owner can enter today’s menu, price, stock, or promotion, and the AI generates natural spoken messages that can be played automatically through a phone speaker or external speaker.

The goal is simple: help small shops sell more without needing extra staff at the front of the store.

---

### Problem

Many small shops, food stalls, cafés, kiosks, and market sellers rely on staff to call customers, explain promotions, and repeat the same sales message many times per day. This creates several problems:

- Staff get tired from repeating the same message.
- Shops may lose passing customers because nobody speaks at the right moment.
- Printed signs are passive and easy to ignore.
- Small shops do not have affordable AI sales automation tools.
- Existing motion speaker devices only play fixed recorded sounds and do not understand menu, promotion, time, or stock.

Small businesses need a simple, low-cost tool that can help promote products automatically in a natural and friendly way.

---

### Solution

HerHelp SpeakShop AI provides an AI voice promotion system for physical shops.

A shop owner can use an old Android phone, tablet, or dedicated speaker device. The device is placed near the front of the shop and connected to the internet. The owner enters menu items, prices, promotions, or product highlights into the HerHelp web app. AI then creates natural sales scripts and converts them into voice messages.

When a customer walks past or enters the detection zone, the device plays a suitable voice message through the phone speaker, Bluetooth speaker, AUX speaker, or external amplifier.

Example message:

“Hello, today we have freshly grilled lemongrass chicken with homemade dipping sauce. Please stop by and try it.”

For Thai shops, the system can generate natural Thai voice messages such as:

“สวัสดีค่ะ วันนี้มีไก่ย่างหมักตะไคร้หอม ๆ น้ำจิ้มทำเองนะคะ แวะชิมก่อนได้นะคะ”

### Core Features

AI Sales Script Generator  
Creates natural promotional messages from menu items, prices, stock, and daily offers.

AI Voice Generator  
Turns generated text into realistic voice messages that can be played automatically.

Mobile Reception Mode  
Uses an old phone or tablet as the camera, internet device, screen, and speaker controller.

Smart Trigger System  
Detects motion or people near the shop and plays a message only when appropriate.

Sound Pack Scheduling  
Prepares different messages for morning, lunch, evening, weekends, promotions, and low-stock situations.

External Speaker Support  
Works with built-in phone speakers, Bluetooth speakers, AUX speakers, USB-C speakers, or shop amplifiers.

Cooldown Control  
Prevents the system from speaking too often and disturbing customers or nearby shops.

ShopOS AI Integration  
Can later connect with QR menu, queue POS, stock, loyalty, customer profile, and promotion systems.

---

### Product Architecture

HerHelp SpeakShop AI uses a simple cloud-and-device model.

HerHelp Cloud acts as the brain. It manages shop data, AI script generation, AI voice generation, sound packs, schedules, settings, and analytics.

Mobile Device or Speaker Device acts as the front-shop execution device. It detects people, plays prepared voice messages, and reports simple activity logs back to HerHelp.

The best MVP approach is to generate voice messages before the shop opens, store them as a daily sound pack, and let the device play them instantly during the day. This avoids slow live AI response and reduces API cost.

The main advantage of SpeakShop AI is simplicity.

Traditional systems require CCTV setup, Linux installation, RTSP configuration, and separate speaker wiring. SpeakShop AI can start with only a mobile phone and an internet connection.

The positioning is:

“Turn any old phone into an AI front-shop promoter.”

This makes the product easier to adopt, easier to explain, and easier to sell to small businesses.
