# HerHelp SheetLayer AI

## One-Page Product Paper

### Overview

**HerHelp SheetLayer AI** is an AI-powered sheet operating layer that helps small businesses turn messy Google Sheets and Excel files into organized dashboards, role-safe views, workflows, sync systems, and LINE-ready business tools.

Many SMEs already use spreadsheets as their lightweight CRM, ERP, MRP, POS support system, order tracker, customer list, product list, registration tool, stock record, and daily report system. These sheets often contain formulas, business logic, confidential customer data, product costs, staff data, margins, payment records, and daily operational files.

The problem is not that SMEs have no data. The problem is that their existing data is scattered, sensitive, difficult to monitor, and hard to safely turn into usable business systems.

**SheetLayer AI solves this by keeping the familiar spreadsheet workflow, while adding AI-assisted mapping, dashboards, permissions, workflows, and business-ready views on top.**

## Product Modules

### 1. SheetMap

**SheetMap** uses AI to understand the structure and business meaning of existing sheets.

It maps:

- columns
- tabs
- formulas
- daily files
- data types
- business purpose
- relationships between sheets
- sensitive fields

Example mapping:

| User Sheet Column | SheetLayer AI Field |
|---|---|
| Customer Name | customer_name |
| Phone | phone_number |
| Product | product_name |
| Payment Status | payment_status |
| Note | order_note |

### 2. SheetView

**SheetView** turns mapped sheet data into clean dashboards and usable views.

It creates:

- owner dashboards
- staff tables
- customer-facing views
- mobile views
- LINE-ready pages
- monitoring dashboards
- KPI cards
- daily summary views

Example role-based views:

- owner sees revenue, cost, margin, stock, and staff activity
- manager sees orders, stock, and operational tasks
- staff sees only today’s assigned work
- customer sees only their form, order, booking, or status page

### 3. SheetFlow

**SheetFlow** turns spreadsheet records into workflow actions.

It supports:

- order status updates
- booking confirmation
- payment follow-up
- task assignment
- approval flow
- reminders
- cancellation review
- staff actions
- customer follow-up

Instead of keeping sheet rows as passive records, SheetFlow turns them into active business workflows.

### 4. SheetSync

**SheetSync** handles sheet updates, imports, exports, and daily sheet operations.

It supports:

- Google Sheets connection
- Excel import
- CSV upload
- Google Drive folder sync
- daily sheet detection
- recurring imports
- mapping memory
- change tracking
- conflict handling
- source history

This is important because many SMEs do not have one perfect master sheet. They often create new sheets daily, weekly, by staff, by campaign, by branch, or by order channel.

SheetSync helps turn many scattered files into one continuous business system.

### 5. SheetGuard

**SheetGuard** protects confidential business data.

It manages:

- role-based access
- field-level visibility
- sensitive field detection
- masked fields
- AI access control
- export control
- audit logs
- owner/staff permissions

Example permission logic:

| Field | Owner | Manager | Staff | AI |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Customer Name | Yes | Yes | Yes | Optional |
| Phone Number | Yes | Yes | Masked | Optional |
| Address | Yes | Limited | Limited | No by default |
| Product Cost | Yes | No | No | No |
| Margin | Yes | No | No | No |

SheetGuard is one of SheetLayer AI’s strongest differentiators because it makes AI and dashboards safer for real business data.
