# ZAGA Districts — Telegram-Native Cyberpunk Community MMORPG Paper

## 1. Executive Summary

**ZAGA Districts** is a standalone game inside the wider **ZAGA ecosystem**.

It is a **Telegram-native 2.5D stylized cyberpunk MMORPG and community city-builder** where real Telegram groups become playable gangs, build their own cyberpunk cities, manage economies, hire NPCs, use token and NFT utility, defend against rivals, and compete for influence across a persistent AI-driven world.

The core idea is simple:

> **Your Telegram group becomes a living cyberpunk city.**

ZAGA Districts is designed for crypto-native communities, Telegram groups, Web3 projects, NFT collections, meme communities, gaming guilds, and social groups that want more than a chat room. Each group can create a city, recruit members, build infrastructure, fund a treasury, run internal quests, manage NPC workers, control internal item prices, integrate digital assets, and defend against other cities.

The game combines:

- Telegram group communities
- Cyberpunk city-building
- Torn-style long-term MMORPG systems
- Community recruitment loops
- City management and NPC salaries
- Token and NFT utility
- AI-driven world events
- Dynamic NPC quests
- Faction warfare and city defense
- 2.5D stylized cyberpunk visuals

ZAGA Districts is not positioned as a casino, pure play-to-earn game, or tap-to-earn mini app. It is positioned as a **community-first MMORPG** where digital assets support identity, access, utility, contribution, and reward systems.

## 3. Core Fantasy

The main fantasy of ZAGA Districts is not only individual character progression. The main fantasy is **community ownership**.

A Telegram group can become a city.

- defending against attacks

- competing against other cities

The city grows because members participate.

## 5. Telegram Group to Gang City System

The foundation of ZAGA Districts is the **Telegram Group to Gang City System**.

### Flow

1. A Telegram group invites the ZAGA Districts bot.
2. The bot verifies the group and its admins.
3. The group creates a gang identity.
4. The gang creates a city.
5. Members connect their Telegram accounts.
6. Members can optionally verify wallets.
7. The group becomes a playable community city.

### The Telegram Group Becomes

- social headquarters
- recruitment channel
- city announcement feed
- war alert channel
- governance layer
- quest notification center
- community identity hub

This system makes ZAGA Districts naturally Telegram-native. It does not force players to form artificial guilds inside a UI. It converts existing communities into playable factions.

## 6. Gang City System

Each gang can create and develop its own cyberpunk city.

A gang city is a private or semi-public city controlled by one Telegram community.

### Each City Has

- city name
- city lord
- officers/admins
- members
- city level
- treasury
- resources
- buildings
- NPC workforce
- defenses
- internal economy
- quests
- taxes
- market settings
- token/NFT utility
- city satisfaction
- war readiness
- city reputation

### City Access Modes

A city can be configured as:

- **Private City** — members only
- **Public City** — visitors allowed
- **Alliance City** — allied groups allowed

This lets group owners control how their city interacts with the wider game world.

## 9. NPC Salaries and Maintenance

NPCs require salaries and resources.

This creates the city’s economic pressure.

### Example Salary Model

| NPC | Salary Cost | Resource Cost |
|---|---:|---:|
| Soldier | 50 credits/day | 2 supplies/day |
| Healer | 120 credits/day | 5 medical kits/day |
| Blacksmith | 150 credits/day | 5 metal/day |
| Engineer | 180 credits/day | 5 repair parts/day |
| Recruiter | 100 credits/day | 2 influence/day |
| Hero Captain | 500 credits/day | 10 energy cores/day |

### Unpaid NPC Degradation

If the city cannot pay NPCs:

1. Warning
2. NPC efficiency reduced
3. NPC service slowed
4. NPC becomes inactive
5. NPC may leave after prolonged non-payment

This creates retention because members must keep the city alive together.

## 11. City Tax and Internal Pricing

City lords can set taxes and internal item prices.

### Taxes Can Apply To

- mission rewards
- marketplace trades
- internal shop purchases
- city jobs
- war rewards

### Tax Strategy

- 0% tax: member-friendly, slower growth
- 5% tax: balanced growth
- 10% tax: faster upgrades, possible member complaints
- 20% tax: aggressive war economy, high dissatisfaction risk

### Internal Market Control

Each city can control prices inside its own market.

Example:

- Health Pack: 20 ZAGA Credits
- Plasma Ammo: 5 group tokens
- Shield Battery: NFT-holder access
- War Ticket: 100 group tokens

Price control requires guardrails:

- minimum and maximum price ranges
- cooldowns on price changes
- economy logs
- anti-arbitrage controls
- treasury transparency

- NPC services are active

## 14. City Defense and War System

Gang cities can be attacked by rival gangs.

City lords must prepare defenses.

### Defense Structures

| Defense | Function |
|---|---|
| Wall Gate | Slows attackers |
| Defense Tower | Attacks enemies |
| Drone Nest | Sends AI drones |
| Shield Generator | Absorbs damage |
| EMP Trap | Disables attacker skills |
| Minefield | Area damage |
| Firewall Node | Blocks hacking attacks |
| Radar Tower | Early warning |
| Decoy Terminal | Misleads attackers |
| Repair Station | Repairs structures |
| Sniper Nest | Long-range defense |
| Anti-Boss Cannon | Protects against raid bosses |

### Attack Types

| Attack Type | Risk | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| Scout Mission | Low | Gather information |
| Data Hack | Medium | Steal minor resources or intel |
| Resource Raid | Medium | Steal materials |
| Tower Assault | High | Damage defenses |
| Full City War | Very High | Major faction conflict |
| Siege Event | Extreme | Seasonal large-scale conflict |

### War Flow

1. Attacker declares raid or war.
2. Defender receives Telegram alert.
3. Preparation window starts.
4. Defender activates towers, shields, soldiers, and heroes.
5. Attack map opens.
6. Members defend or attack.
7. City structures may take damage.
8. Rewards and reputation are distributed.
9. City enters repair or cooldown phase.

### Fairness Protection

The war system needs:

- attack limits
- war windows
- newbie protection
- city shield cooldowns
- repair periods
- matchmaking rules
- anti-bullying controls
- anti-whale domination mechanics

The chosen visual style is:

> **2.5D stylized cyberpunk**

### Visual Principles

- low-poly-inspired 2.5D look
- isometric/top-down maps
- neon lighting
- holographic UI
- strong silhouettes
- modular district assets
- lightweight VFX
- readable mobile combat and movement
- optimized for Telegram Mini App

### Visual Goal

> **Looks premium, loads fast.**

ZAGA Districts should avoid heavy 3D MMORPG graphics. It should use lightweight 2.5D visuals that create a strong cyberpunk atmosphere without large downloads or poor mobile performance.

## 18. Telegram Stars and Game Currency

Telegram Stars should be used as a one-way purchase path.

> **Stars → Game Currency**

The basic in-game currency should not convert back into Stars for players.

### Economy Layers

| Layer | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Telegram Stars | Purchase game currency |
| Game Credits | Basic game spending |
| City Resources | Buildings, salaries, upgrades |
| Tokens/NFTs | Utility, access, identity, rewards |
| Smart Contracts | Future treasury/reward settlement |

This separates basic game spending from digital asset rewards and reduces risk.

## 21. Multi-Group Membership

A Telegram user may belong to many groups.

The recommended rule is:

> **Players may belong to many communities, but only one primary faction counts per season.**

Players can:

- visit public cities
- view multiple group communities
- socialize with many gangs
- act as limited mercenaries

But only one primary gang counts for:

- war rewards
- seasonal rewards
- faction leaderboard
- city contribution score
- territory influence

This prevents farming, spying, and reward abuse.
