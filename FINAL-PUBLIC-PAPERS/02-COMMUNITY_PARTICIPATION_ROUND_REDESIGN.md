# Community Participation Round Redesign

## Executive Summary

FUZE redesigns the earlier presale-style structure into a **Community Participation Round** aligned with FUZE v2’s product-first platform direction.

The Community Participation Round moves FUZE away from price-tier, discount-driven, token-first messaging and toward a product-aligned, compliance-aware, transparent participation system for eligible community members, real users, contributors, and long-term ecosystem supporters.

FUZE keeps the full **110,000,000 FUZE Community Participation Allocation** within the fixed **500,000,000 FUZE** total supply. FUZE organizes this allocation into clearer participation purposes connected to community access, product-user participation, contributor recognition, and future community programs.

FUZE does not present the Community Participation Round through TGE multiple framing, guaranteed upside, discount-to-TGE language, buy-before-listing messaging, ROI framing, guaranteed liquidity, or listing promises.

## 1. FUZE Position

FUZE uses **Community Participation Round** as the public-facing name for the redesigned community access model.

FUZE uses **Community Participation Allocation** when referring to the full **110,000,000 FUZE** community allocation inside the total FUZE tokenomics structure.

FUZE retires the earlier public framing based on four price-first tiers:

| Old Round | Allocation Logic | Price | Public Framing Risk |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Pioneer Round | First 15,000,000 FUZE | $0.015 | High discount framing |
| Builder Round | Next 25,000,000 FUZE | $0.025 | Price-first purchase framing |
| Architect Round | Next 30,000,000 FUZE | $0.035 | Speculation-oriented framing |
| Vanguard Round | Remaining tokens | $0.045 | TGE-price anchoring |
| TGE Reference Price | N/A | $0.055 | Expected upside framing |

FUZE replaces that model with a public participation structure built around:

- product readiness
- platform readiness
- legal and compliance review
- eligibility rules
- controlled circulation
- fair treatment of existing participant records, where applicable
- transparent round status
- clear unlock and claim rules
- separation from the Private Strategic Seed Round
- separation from Platform Credits and future stablecoin payout systems

FUZE position:

**The Community Participation Round is a product-aligned, compliance-aware, transparent participation system for eligible community members, real users, contributors, and long-term ecosystem supporters.**

## 2. Platform Context

FUZE is a transparency-first AI SaaS platform building practical products on top of shared infrastructure for identity, credits, payments, AI orchestration, reporting, and ecosystem participation.

FUZE execution order is:

**Product usage first. Platform rails second. Broader ecosystem participation after that.**

The Community Participation Round follows this execution order. FUZE does not treat community access as a short-term token sale mechanic. FUZE connects community participation to visible product progress, platform rails, legal readiness, smart contract readiness, and public transparency.

FUZE v2 launch focus is **HerHelp.com** and **ZAGA.io**, supported by FUZE Core Platform rails and accelerated internally by **Botmad**.

The Community Participation Round fits the platform in the following way:

| Platform Area | Community Participation Relationship |
|---|---|
| FUZE Core Platform | Provides identity, wallet, claim, eligibility, reporting, and transparency rails |
| HerHelp.com | Product-user participation surface for practical AI SaaS adoption |
| ZAGA.io | Product and ecosystem participation surface connected to token utility and user engagement |
| Platform Credits | Product usage layer, separate from FUZE token participation |
| FUZE token | Ecosystem participation asset on Ethereum mainnet |
| Base operational rails | Credits and future payout-related execution layer, separate from Ethereum FUZE token ownership |
| Transparency dashboard | Public visibility for round status, allocation usage, contract references, and policy records |
| Governance direction | Long-term participation and reporting discipline without overstating live governance control |
| Compliance posture | Eligibility, jurisdiction, contract, pricing, and risk boundaries before public participation opens |

FUZE token is for ecosystem participation. Platform Credits are for product usage. Profit participation is a long-term design direction, not an immediate or guaranteed promise.

FUZE recognizes the following treatment paths where applicable:

| Option | Meaning | Notes |

FUZE handles existing participant records, where applicable, before any new Community Participation Round is launched.
