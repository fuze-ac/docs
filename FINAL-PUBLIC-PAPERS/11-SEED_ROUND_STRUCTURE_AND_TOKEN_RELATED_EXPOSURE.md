# Seed Round Structure and Token-Related Exposure

## Executive Summary

FUZE is preparing a **Private Strategic Seed Round** for strategic investors, operator-angels, and early-stage partners.

The round supports FUZE v2’s movement from specified foundation into product-first execution. The Seed Round is designed to accelerate FUZE v2 product, platform, operations, transparency, and go-to-market readiness, with **HerHelp.com** and **ZAGA.io** as the first major production milestone focus.

FUZE presents the Seed Round as private strategic execution funding, not as a public token sale. Any token-related exposure is handled only as a private, legally reviewed component of the investment structure. FUZE does not publicly present Seed Round participation as a token presale, ICO, pre-TGE sale, public round, Community Participation Round, or listing-driven opportunity.

FUZE’s public Seed Round sentence is:

**FUZE is preparing a Private Strategic Seed Round for strategic investors, operator-angels, and early-stage partners. Full details, structure, and terms are shared privately with qualified interested parties. This is not a public token sale.**

### 3.6 Possible Investment Instruments

Possible structures include:

| Instrument | Description | Notes |
|---|---|---|
