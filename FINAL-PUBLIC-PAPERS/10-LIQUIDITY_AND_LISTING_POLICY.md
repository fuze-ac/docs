# Liquidity and Listing Policy

## Executive Summary

FUZE uses a dedicated **Liquidity & Market Operations** allocation to support responsible market structure.

The allocation is **30,000,000 FUZE**, equal to **6.00%** of the fixed **500,000,000 FUZE** total supply. This allocation supports liquidity, listing readiness, LP seeding, exchange deployment, market-maker inventory, and broader market-structure operations.

FUZE does not treat Liquidity & Market Operations tokens as normal public sale inventory. These tokens are not used for team compensation, advisor compensation, general treasury spending, airdrops, community rewards, unrestricted partner grants, or hidden token distribution.

The Liquidity and Listing Policy supports FUZE v2’s Controlled Circulation Policy by ensuring liquidity tokens move only through approved market-structure policies, with public transparency where safe and confidentiality where operationally necessary.

FUZE does not guarantee exchange listing, listing timing, liquidity, price performance, or market outcome.

## 1. FUZE Position

FUZE reserves **30,000,000 FUZE** for Liquidity & Market Operations.

FUZE public rule:

**Liquidity Operations tokens are for liquidity, listing, LP, exchange, market-maker, and market-structure support. They are not normal sale inventory.**

FUZE uses this allocation for controlled operational deployment only.

| Item | FUZE Position |
|---|---|

| Purpose | Liquidity and market-structure support |
| Public buy access | Usually no |

| Not for | Public sale, team compensation, advisor compensation, airdrops, general treasury spending |
| Control model | Multisig, policy, approval, event logs, reporting where safe |
| Public promise | No listing date promise, no price promise, no liquidity guarantee |

FUZE principle:

**The Liquidity Operations Vault is designed for responsible market-structure support, not discretionary selling.**

FUZE uses the Liquidity Operations allocation only when it supports a defined liquidity, listing, exchange, LP, or market-structure function.

- LP pools
- exchange listing support
- market-maker operations
- liquidity depth
- market stabilization
- future venue deployment

FUZE rule:

**Liquidity Operations tokens are not available through normal public vault purchase windows.**

| Market-maker structured allocation | Contract-bound and operationally necessary |
