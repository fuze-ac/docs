# Platform Credits Relationship

## Executive Summary

FUZE uses **Platform Credits** as the internal product usage rail for FUZE products and services.

The FUZE token and Platform Credits are separate systems. FUZE token is for long-term ecosystem participation, holder alignment, product-linked benefits, participation eligibility, governance readiness, and future ecosystem utility. Platform Credits are for AI usage, SaaS features, tools, workflows, game actions, automations, subscriptions, and platform services.

This distinction is central to FUZE v2 because FUZE is a transparency-first AI SaaS platform with practical products such as **HerHelp.com** and **ZAGA.io**, with future expansion into ToolGrid, Botmad, QTB, AIMM, AIE, and additional AI SaaS services.

FUZE applies the principle:

**FUZE token is for long-term ecosystem participation. Platform Credits are for product usage.**

Platform Credits are not FUZE tokens, not investment assets, not payout assets, not stablecoins, not governance tokens, not profit participation claims, and not exchange listing assets.

## 1. FUZE Position

FUZE keeps FUZE token and Platform Credits as separate systems.

FUZE token is positioned as:

**The ecosystem participation asset for long-term holder alignment, product-linked benefits, participation eligibility, governance readiness, and future ecosystem utility.**

Platform Credits are positioned as:

**The internal product usage rail for AI usage, SaaS features, tools, workflows, games, automations, subscriptions, and platform services.**

Stablecoin payout systems, if implemented later, are positioned as:

**A separate future profit participation framework, subject to legal, accounting, treasury, technical, and transparency controls.**

FUZE economic role separation:

| Layer | Asset / System | Primary Role |
|---|---|---|
| Ecosystem participation layer | FUZE token | Holder alignment, ecosystem participation, eligibility source, future governance readiness |
| Internal consumption layer | Platform Credits | Product usage, subscriptions, AI usage, tools, workflows, game actions, services |
| Compensation layer | USDT / USDC / fiat-equivalent | Team, advisor, contributor, partner, contractor, and operational compensation |
| Future participation layer | Stablecoin payout system | Future profit participation framework, subject to review |
| Product revenue layer | Fiat, stablecoins, payment processors, platform billing | Business revenue from products and services |

FUZE position:

**FUZE token is not the internal spending unit for every product action. Platform Credits handle product usage and billing.**

FUZE execution order is:

Platform Credits support this order because they create a simple usage and billing system for real product activity before broader token-based ecosystem participation expands.

## 4. Investor and Community Relevance

The Platform Credits relationship matters because it makes FUZE easier to use, easier to explain, and easier to scale.

For strategic partners, credits create a clean system for usage packages, partner-funded credits, campaigns, integrations, and product monetization.

FUZE is not relying on token speculation as the only exit path. FUZE is building real products, real users, revenue potential, shared platform rails, and strategic acquisition optionality.
