# Legal and Compliance Messaging

## Executive Summary

FUZE uses legal and compliance-aware messaging to keep its public communication clear, product-first, transparency-first, and responsible.

FUZE v2 includes several sensitive topics: Private Strategic Seed Round, token-related exposure, Community Participation Round, Public Vault Access System, Controlled Circulation Policy, stablecoin compensation, token utility, Platform Credits, future profit participation, liquidity and listing readiness, governance, and treasury controls.

FUZE communicates these topics with careful boundaries. FUZE does not present fundraising as a public token sale. FUZE does not present token utility as guaranteed income. FUZE does not present profit participation as immediate or guaranteed. FUZE does not present Platform Credits as investment assets. FUZE does not present listing readiness as a listing promise.

FUZE public stance:

**FUZE v2 is product-first, transparency-first, and compliance-aware. The ecosystem vision remains, and execution and communication remain responsible.**

## 2. Platform Context

FUZE is a transparency-first AI SaaS platform building practical products on top of shared infrastructure for identity, credits, payments, AI orchestration, reporting, and ecosystem participation.

FUZE execution order is:

**Product usage first. Platform rails second. Broader ecosystem participation after that.**

Legal and compliance messaging supports this execution order by keeping public communication focused on product delivery, platform rails, transparency, and responsible ecosystem participation.

FUZE v2 launch focus is **HerHelp.com** and **ZAGA.io**, supported by FUZE Core Platform rails and accelerated internally by **Botmad**.

Legal and compliance messaging connects to FUZE platform layers in the following way:

| Platform Area | Legal and Compliance Messaging Relationship |
|---|---|
| FUZE Core Platform | Provides identity, credits, payments, reporting, governance, transparency, and public-read systems that require clear legal boundaries |
| HerHelp.com | Web2-facing AI SaaS product requiring product-first, non-crypto-confusing messaging for SME users |
| ZAGA.io | Token utility and community ecosystem surface requiring careful utility, game, reward, and participation language |

| Platform Credits | Product usage system that must remain separate from investment, payout, and token claims |
| Private Strategic Seed Round | Private fundraising process that remains separate from public token sale framing |

| Public Vault Access System | Future access system requiring eligibility, jurisdiction, compliance, pricing, lockup, and risk boundaries |
| Profit participation | Long-term design direction, not immediate and not guaranteed |
| Governance model | Multisig, timelock, policy registry, public reporting, and future DAO-lite readiness with clear boundaries |

FUZE token is for ecosystem participation. Platform Credits are for product usage. Stablecoin compensation is for work payment. Profit participation is a long-term design direction, not an immediate or guaranteed promise.
